Far Cry 3 (2012)

Far Cry 3 was developed by Ubisoft Montreal using feedback from Far Cry 2, and published by Ubisoft for
Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 3, and Xbox 360 on November 29, 2012 in Australia, November 30 in Europe,
and December 4 in North America. It was later re-released on the PlayStation 4 and Xbox One on June 26, 2018.
